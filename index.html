Who I am
About me

I'm pursuing two degrees simultaneously — a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering at SVNIT Surat and a B.S in Electronic Systems at IIT Madras. My work sits at the overlap of aerospace materials, computational modeling, and intelligent systems.

On the engineering side, I've done hands-on work in quality control and NDT at Godrej Aerospace and HAL — studying how aerospace-grade titanium behaves under load and how manufacturing constraints shape real components.

On the computational side, I build things: ML pipelines that predict weld properties from images, Python-based flow solvers, real-time tracking robots. I like problems that need both domain knowledge and software to solve.

Outside engineering — I'm a certified Sangeet Visharad, a Bachelor's in Hindustani Classical Music. Ragas are just another kind of system.
